The orbit-and-spark mark. Use the white mark on cosmic/dark surfaces, the ink mark on light. 2000×2000 PNG, transparent.

Do
  • Give the mark clear space equal to its height.
  • Use the white mark on cosmos, ink mark on daybreak.
  • Place it over the cosmos wash or a solid brand color.
Don't
  • Don't recolor, stretch or rotate the mark.
  • Don't place the ink mark on dark or busy imagery.
  • Don't add drop shadows or outlines to the wordmark.
